# Branding Direction

## Working Title
Editable PDF

## Logo Direction
Use a simple centered document glyph with a subtle cutout or sparkle mark to suggest OCR and transformation.

## App Icon Rules
- Keep the mark readable at small sizes.
- Use one primary accent color and one neutral background.
- Avoid complex details, thin strokes, or text in the icon.
- Prepare light, dark, clear, and tinted variants.

## Screenshot Tone
- Clean white background.
- One strong accent color.
- Large spacing, minimal controls, high contrast.
